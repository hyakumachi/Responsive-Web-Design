Lewis Hamilton Tribute Page

Sir Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton (born 7 January 1985) is a British racing driver who competes in Formula One for Ferrari. Hamilton has won a joint-record seven Formula One World Drivers' Championship titles—tied with Michael Schumacher—and holds the records for most wins (105), pole positions (104), and podium finishes (202), among others.

Career Timeline

  • 2007 - F1 debut with McLaren, finished 2nd in championship
  • 2008 - Won first world title with McLaren
  • 2013 - Moved to Mercedes
  • 2014 - Won world title with Mercedes
  • 2015 - Won second straight title with Mercedes
  • 2017 - Won fourth world title
  • 2018 - Won fifth world title
  • 2019 - Won sixth world title
  • 2020 - Won seventh world title, tied Schumacher
  • 2021 - Lost title to Verstappen in final race
  • 2022 - No wins, Mercedes struggles
  • 2023 - Still with Mercedes, no title
  • 2025 - Set to race for Ferrari
